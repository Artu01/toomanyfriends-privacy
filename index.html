Privacy Policy – Too Many Friends

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Too Many Friends ("the App") is a personal contact manager that helps you keep track of friends, birthdays, and events. This policy explains how your information is handled.

1. Data We Collect

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Names, birthdays, and profile photos you add manually.
  • Dates of meetings and personal events you record.
  • Your notification preferences.

No data is transmitted to external servers or the developer.

2. Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – to send local reminders (birthdays, events, reconnect alerts). All notifications are scheduled and fired entirely on-device.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – to let you pick a photo from your gallery as a profile picture. The App reads only the image you select; it does not scan or upload your photo library.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data you enter is used solely to display your contacts and to schedule local notifications on your behalf. Nothing is analysed, aggregated, or shared.

4. Affiliate Links – Amazon

The App's gift-suggestion feature opens Amazon.com (amazon.com) in your device's browser with search queries based on the interests you have entered for a contact.

These links include an affiliate tag (idpromoter-21), which means the developer may receive a small commission if you make a purchase after following the link, at no extra cost to you.

Once you leave the App and land on Amazon, Amazon's own Privacy Notice governs how your data is handled. The App itself does not receive any information about your activity on Amazon.

5. Third-Party Services

Other than the Amazon affiliate redirect described above, the App does not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or any other third-party service that accesses your data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored in the app's private storage on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the App, which permanently removes every file the App has created.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
